Who We Are

Rooted in quality, grown in trust.

For over four decades, Oakridge Timber Co. has been the trusted source for premium hardwoods and softwoods. We work directly with sustainable forests, ensuring every plank meets the highest standards of quality and environmental responsibility.

From structural beams to fine furniture-grade boards, our range covers every need — with expert grading, kiln-drying, and bespoke milling on request.
